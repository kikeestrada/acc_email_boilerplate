Did You Know?

 
Even the 2X points you earn at gas stations and supermarkets (for the first $6,000 per year) get rounded up to the nearest 10 points!
 
For example: Fill up your tank for $31 and earn 70 points!

($31 x 2 = 62 points, which round up to 70)
